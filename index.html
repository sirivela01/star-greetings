Barakatta Rules

Objective

Race all 6 of your colored rock tokens from your starting square, around the outer loop counter-clockwise, then clockwise through the inner loop to land precisely in the center home square. The first to get all 6 rocks home wins!

Rolling & Entering

  • Roll 6: You enter all 6 rocks at once onto your starting square (marked with X) and get an extra roll.
  • Roll 1: You can choose to enter exactly 1 rock from your yard OR move a rock on the board.
  • Roll 2, 3, 4, 5: Move any of your active on-board rocks by that many steps. If no rocks are on the board, your turn is skipped.

Movement & Capturing

  • Movement Path: Travel the outer track counter-clockwise. Once a capture has been made, your rocks can enter the inner loop to go clockwise towards the center.
  • Capture Lock: To enter the inner loop home stretch, you must capture (cut) at least one opponent rock during the match.
  • Capturing: Land on an opponent rock on any non-X square to capture it, send it back to their yard, and earn an extra roll!
  • Safe Squares (X): Starting cells and corners are safe zones. Captures cannot happen here, and multiple players can co-exist.
